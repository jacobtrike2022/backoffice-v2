## Platform Overview

Trike Backoffice 2.0 is an enterprise-grade operations and learning management platform purpose-built for convenience store and foodservice organizations. It provides a single, unified hub for training, compliance, operational procedures, knowledge management, and workforce communications — all organized around the way real c-store businesses operate.

### Who It's For

| Role | What They Get |
|------|--------------|
| **Organization Leaders / Owners** | Full visibility into training completion, compliance status, and operational performance across every store and district |
| **District Managers** | Oversight of multiple store locations with drill-down reporting and assignment management |
| **Store Managers** | Day-to-day tools for managing team training, tracking certifications, and running store operations |
| **Team Members** | Easy access to training content, operational guides, forms, and the company knowledge base |

### Organizational Hierarchy

The platform mirrors your real business structure:

- **Organization** (your company)
  - **Districts** (regional groupings)
    - **Stores** (individual locations)
      - **Team Members** (employees at each store)

Every piece of content, every report, every compliance requirement flows through this hierarchy — so the right people see the right information at the right level.
